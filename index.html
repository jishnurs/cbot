Why Use CBOT?

CBOT uses modern front-end technologies and is packed with useful components and widgets to speed up your app development

UX-Centered Design

Bespoke UX/UI design to enhance your app's user experience. Let CBOT take care of the design so you can develop without design resources.

Supercharge App Development

CBOT provides developers with a ready-made front-end solution so you can concentrate on making your app’s back-end robust and awesome. It empowers small teams to create big things.

Mobile First

Built on the popular Bootstrap framework, so the design is “Mobile First” and fully responsive. For a startup, building an HTML5 webapp which also works across mobile devices is the most cost-effective way to get your product off the ground.

Our Team

See who are behind CBOT

CBOT is created by Deepak R C and Sujith S. Deepak and Sujith got to know each other while working as freelancers on Google projects and became good friends. They firmly believe with the right resource, solopreneurs and small teams can execute beautiful products too. Thus they made CBOT to help developers and startups make outstanding products - the internet has made it possible for the "small guys" to compete directly with the "big guys".
